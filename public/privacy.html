At MBJ International, we value your trust. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you visit our website. By using our website, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

We collect personal details when you place an order, fill out the contact form, or communicate with us. This may include:

  • Name, Email address, Phone number
  • Billing & shipping address
  • Order/payment details (handled securely by third-party gateways)
Note: We do not store your full credit/debit card information.

b. Non-Personal Information

We may collect automatic data such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, cookies and tracking information, and user behavior data for improving website experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to process and deliver orders, provide customer support, improve website performance, send order updates (only if you opt-in), analyze user behavior, and comply with legal requirements.

3. Sharing of Information

We do NOT sell or rent your personal data. We may share your information only with payment processors, courier & logistics partners, service providers, and legal authorities when required by law. All partners follow strict confidentiality standards.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, alteration, and cyber threats. While we take strong precautions, no online platform is 100% secure.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies to enhance browsing experience, remember user preferences, and analyze website performance. You can disable cookies from your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their content, privacy practices, or security. Please review their policies before providing personal information.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data, opt out of marketing emails, and request a copy of the data we hold. To exercise these rights, contact us at: info@mbj-international.com

8. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary for fulfilling orders, complying with legal obligations, and accounting purposes. After this period, your data is securely deleted.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

MBJ International may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted on this page and the "Last Updated" date will be revised.
