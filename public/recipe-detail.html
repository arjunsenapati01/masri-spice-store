Classic Paneer Tikka Masala

⏱ 45 mins 👨‍🍳 Medium 🍽️ 4 Servings ⭐ Feat: Maa श्री Garam Masala

Ingredients

  • 250g Paneer (Indian Cottage Cheese)
  • 1 cup Thick Yogurt (Curd)
  • 2 tbsp Maa श्री Garam Masala
  • 1 tbsp Maa श्री Kasuri Methi
  • 2 Large Onions, pureed
  • 3 Tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 2 tbsp Fresh Cream
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh Coriander for garnish

Instructions

1
Marinate the Paneer: In a large mixing bowl, combine the thick yogurt, 1 tbsp of Maa श्री Garam Masala, a pinch of salt, and a splash of mustard oil. Toss the cubed paneer into the marinade and let it rest for at least 30 minutes.
2
Grill the Paneer: Thread the marinated paneer onto skewers and grill or pan-fry them until they develop a beautiful golden char on all sides. Set aside.
3
Prepare the Gravy: Heat oil or ghee in a heavy-bottomed pan. Add the onion puree and sauté until golden brown. Stir in the ginger-garlic paste and cook for another minute until fragrant.
4
Simmer with Spices: Pour in the tomato puree. Cook until the oil begins to separate from the masala. Now, add the remaining 1 tbsp of Maa श्री Garam Masala. The premium blend will instantly elevate the aroma of your kitchen.
5
Finish the Dish: Add half a cup of water and bring the gravy to a gentle simmer. Gently drop the grilled paneer cubes into the sauce. Crush the Maa श्री Kasuri Methi between your palms and sprinkle it over the top. Swirl in the fresh cream, garnish with coriander, and serve hot with Naan or Jeera Rice!
